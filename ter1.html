About the Club

Team Endurance Racing is a SAE Collegiate club established in 2009, dedicated to designing, building, and racing high-performance All-Terrain vehicles. With a strong focus on innovation, engineering excellence, and teamwork, the club actively participates in BAJA competitions, pushing the boundaries of vehicle dynamics and endurance.

Objective of the Club.

Aims to design, build, and optimize high-performance all-terrain vehicles for SAE BAJA competitions. Our goal is to enhance technical expertise, foster innovation, and develop industry-ready engineers through hands-on experience in automotive design, fabrication, and testing.

Vision & Mission

To be a leading student motorsport team that pioneers innovation in all-terrain vehicle engineering while shaping future automotive professionals through real-world challenges and competitions.
  • To provide a platform for students to apply engineering concepts in vehicle design and manufacturing.
  • To continuously improve vehicle performance through research, testing, and innovation.
  • To cultivate teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills essential for the automotive industry.
  • To compete at the highest level in BAJA events, showcasing technical excellence and endurance.

Activities

  • Vehicle design and manufacturing
  • Research, testing, and innovation
  • Community Projects
