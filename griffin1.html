About the Club

Introduction:

We are an Aeromodelling team which participates in multiple National level Competitions like SAE DDC and SAE Aerothon.

Our Mission:

is to spark passion and creativity in students by providing them with advanced skills and hands-on experience to transform the fields of aeromodelling and aviation. Through workshops and exciting competitions like the SAE DDC, and SAE Aerothon, we aim to nurture innovation, teamwork, and excellence. We are committed to achieving AIR 1 in both SAE DDC and SAE Aerothon 2025 by putting in our best efforts and ensuring the team gains valuable learning and practical experience.
To maximize the potential of our team by fostering growth, collaboration, and hands-on expertise in aeromodelling and multirotor technology. Our vision is to pass down this knowledge to future generations, ensuring a legacy of excellence, and to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of India’s aviation community.

Activities

  • Weekly Workshops
  • Learning and Training
  • RC plane and drone building
